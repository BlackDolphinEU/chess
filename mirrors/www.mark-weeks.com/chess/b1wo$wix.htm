  • 2010-12-25: • Hou Yifan is a new World Champion! 'The awarding ceremony of the World Championship took place in Anemon Hotel in Antakya on 24th of December. The same day the new world Champion was determined on the tie-break match between two Chinese players Hou Yifan and Ruan Lufei.'

  • 2011-03-06: Humpy Koneru is a winner of the 6th Women's FIDE Grand Prix 2011 • 'Women's FIDE Grand Prix in Doha has come to its end. It was an exciting battle of chess players. Humpy Koneru became a winner of the competition and also got the right to play the match against Hou Yifan. Elina Danielian and Marie Sebag are in the second and the third places respectively.'

  • 2011-03-22 Bidding procedure and regulations for the Women's World Championship Match 2011 • 'FIDE is publishing the bidding procedure and regulations for the Women's World Championship Match 2011 between World Champion Hou Yifan (China) and challenger Humpy Koneru (India). The match is scheduled for 10 November to 5 December 2011 and the deadline for receiving bids is 15 June.'

  • 2011-08-09: World Championships Matches - Press Release • 'FIDE is further pleased to announce that the Women’s World Chess Championship Match between Women World Champion Hou Yifan from China and the Challenger Humpy Koneru from India, will be held in Tirana, Albania in November 2011 for a prize fund of 200,000 euros.'
