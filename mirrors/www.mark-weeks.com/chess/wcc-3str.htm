World Chess Championship
Three star games

As a result of our researches into chess literature, we have linked many World Championship games from our highlights pages to our bibliography page, each link marked with a star. A number of games have received three or more stars, which is an indication of their popularity, importance, or interest to the chess world. These games are referenced on this page.

The links to the right of each game are to the corresponding reference in the highlights page for the event. This places the game in its historical context.

The links to the left of each game marked with a are to a page on Chessgames.com, dedicated to the game, where you can view the game using your browser.
