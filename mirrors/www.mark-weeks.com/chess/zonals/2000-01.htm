2.3 Jalapa (Mexico) 1999-09


angelfire.com/biz2/fenamac

1999 FIDE Zonal 2.3 was played in Jalapa (Mexico) from September 16th to September 25th, 1999. The tournament was won by Cuban chessplayer Jesús Nogueiras (7.5 points in ten rounds by Round Robin system) and he classified to 2000 World FIDE India/Iran. In the second place of 1999 Zonal 2.3 there was a tie between Gilberto Hernandez (MEX), Gildardo García (COL) and Dario Alzate (COL). After the tie-break match (at the beginning of 2000) Mexican Gilberto Hernández got second ticket to 2000 World FIDE Cup in India/Iran. • Source: R.Santana (FE200652)
