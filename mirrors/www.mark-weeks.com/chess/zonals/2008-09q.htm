World Cup 2009: Participant's Information
http://www.fide.com/component/content/article/1-fide-news/4037-list-of-qualifiers-for-world-cup

Thursday, 03 September 2009 07:53

FIDE is announcing the confirmed participants for the World Cup in Khanty-Mansiysk, 20 November - 15 December 2009.

The pairings of the 1st round will be announced on 2 November 2009, immediately after the publishing of the November rating list.

Information about hotels and organising details can also be found on the official website: http://www.ugra-chess.ru/eng/main_e.htm
