WORLD CUP 2017 - PLAYER'S CONTRACT AND QUALIFIERS
http://www.fide.com/component/content/article/1-fide-news/10303-world-cup-2017-players-contract-and-qualifiers.html

Monday, 10 July 2017 09:30

FIDE is publishing the player's contract for the World Cup 2017 to be held in Tbilisi, Georgia from 2 September (player's technical meeting) to 27 September 2017 (tiebreaks/closing ceremony). The qualified players are expected to fax or email scanned the original document of their signed contract to the FIDE Secretariat's fax number or email office@fide.com no later than Monday 17 July 2017, 14:00 GMT.
