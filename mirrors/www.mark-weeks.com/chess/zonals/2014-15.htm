From ACP Wildcard for World Cup 2015 [2014-09-30]:-
The ACP will have a wild card in the World Cup 2015 that will take place from September 10 till October 4 [2015] in Baku, Azerbaijan. The wild card will go to the highest-placed participant of the ACP Tour 2014 who has not qualified by rating, continental championships or any other means. In the last year's World Cup 2013 the ACP wildcard was GM Vachier-Lagrave, who reached the semi-final stage.

The wild card went to no.12 on the list, GM Romain Edouard.
