Qualifiers for the World Cup 2011
http://www.fide.com/component/content/article/1-fide-news/5307-qualifiers-for-the-world-cup-2011.html

Wednesday, 08 June 2011 13:19

http://chess.ugrasport.com/

FIDE is announcing the list of qualifiers for the World Cup in Khanty-Mansiysk, 26 August (arrival) to 21 September 2011 (departure).

All participants should sign and send their player's contract to the FIDE Secretariat (fax no. +30 2109212859) no later than 20 June. Participants who need an entry visa to Russia shall contact the local Organising Committee at the emails [...]@ugrasport.com, [...]@ugrasport.com and [...]@ugrasport.com to receive the necessary travel information, hotel bookings and invitation letter.
