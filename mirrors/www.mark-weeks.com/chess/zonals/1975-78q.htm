GMG notes:-

  • Euwe, Averbakh, Matanovic, Elo and Golombek served on the selection committee. (Thanks to James Pratt).
  • The FIDE selection committee chose Kavalek to be 1st reserve, and he was apparently brought in when the Soviet player Gennadi Kuzmin pulled out.
  • Panno was chosen as first reserve by the FIDE selection committee and when Leonid Stein passed away, was invited to play in his place.
  • The FIDE selection committee also invited Gennadi Kuzmin to play but he seems to have withdrawn. Smyslov seems to have been brought in to balance the number of players.
  • The highly rated Rafael Vaganian came 11th at Vilnius, was selected as eighth reserve, and thus failed to qualify.
