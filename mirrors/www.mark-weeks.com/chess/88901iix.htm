Kavalek withdrew after six rounds; his results are not included in the totals for the other players. R. Huebner was also seeded into the tournament, but declined to participate. This meant that there were two players having free days during each round.

Ribli had a free day during the last round. To show his displeasure, he refused to play a tiebreak game against Tal. The game would have been important if an extra place had opened up for the candidates matches.
