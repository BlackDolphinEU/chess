World Chess Championship
2017 Grand Prix

From FIDE.com:-

  • 2016-12-07: Grand-Prix 2017 Events Venues and Dates confirmed • 'Sharjah, Moscow, Geneva and Palma De Mallorca in Spain will host the four revamped World Chess Grand Prix events in 2017. The new-look Grand Prix series will be contested by 24 players, rather than 12 as had previously been the case, with 18 players in each tournament.'

  • 2016-12-28: FIDE Grand-Prix 2017 Announcement • 'FIDE announces the full details of the Grand-Prix 2017, from which the first two winners will qualify to the Candidates Tournament of the World Chess Championship 2018. The schedule of the four events is the following: [...] In total 24 players will participate, 15 qualifiers as per regulations plus 9 nominees from AGON Ltd.'

From Worldchess.com:-

  • 2017-02-06: List of Players and a Sponsor Announced for Grand Prix • 'The Grand Prix is the first leg in the road to the World Championship; Kaspersky Lab, a global cybersecurity firm, is a sponsor.'
