Chess Informant

Best 10 games each issue (#1-71)

In each issue of Chess Informant, 10 games from the previous issue are spotlighted as 'best games'. Of course, many of these games have been played in World Chess Championship competitions.

Best game of each issue (#1-68)

From the 'best games', these were judged the single 'best game' from the previous issue.

Best 10 games of first 30 years (#1-64); Judges' and Readers' choices

In 1996, Chess Informant ran a competition to determine the best games from its first 30 years of publication. These games were drawn from the best game of each issue and covered issues 1 through 64. These games were selected by the Judges, by the Readers, or by both.

Best 500 games from issues 1-71.

To mark the release of the Chess Informant Reader software, the Chess Informant editors compiled a selection of 500 best games from issues 1 through 71. One game was chosen from each opening classification (A00-E99) and included in the selection. More than 80 games were from World Chess Championship competitions.
