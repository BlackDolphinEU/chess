Tactics!
Solutions to intermediate puzzles #2.

Click here for the puzzles without solutions.

No. 1

Black has several good moves, but the most direct is 1... Re4+. Both the White Knight and Bishop are pinned.

No. 2

1... d4 2. Bxd4 Re2+ (or 2... Bxd4 3. Qxd4 Qg2+) 3. Bxe2 Ne4+

No. 3

1... d3 2. cxd3 Bxe2 3. Qxe2 Qd4+ wins the Bishop.

No. 4

1... Nxh3+ 2. Qxh3 Bxc3 and if 3. Rxc3 Ne2+

No. 5

1... Be7

No. 6

1... Qxh2+ (1... Nf3+ also mates) 2. Kxh2 Nf3+ 3. Kh1 Ng3 mate

No. 7

1... Rxd3 2. cxd3 Bg5
