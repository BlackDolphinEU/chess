King's Indian Defense

1.d4 Nf6 2.c4 g6 3.Nc3 Bg7

Fianchetto Variation

1.d4 Nf6 2.c4 g6 3.Nc3 Bg7 4.Nf3 d6 5.g3 O-O

In most defenses to 1.d4, Black fights tooth and nail to prevent an early e4 by White. In the King's Indian, Black allows e4 and then hits back at the center.
