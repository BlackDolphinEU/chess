Do You Know How the Pieces Move? (Quiz no.3)
Match each piece with a description of its moves.

Choose your selection for a match next to a piece description in the left column. Your choice will be checked automatically in the right column to indicate it has already been used. When you're finished, click Check Your Score to see how well you've done!

Some pieces might seem to have more than one answer. Choose the best answer that allows you to match everything from each column, one to one.

If you need help, see Related Resources at the bottom of this article. If you want to try more move quizzes, see More of this Feature.

You must have JavaScript enabled in your browser.
