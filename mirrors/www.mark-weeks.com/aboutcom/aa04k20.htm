Award Winning Chess Books
Winners of 'Book of the Year' Awards

(Last update October 2012) Looking for a good chess book? How about a great chess book? The books listed below have all won a prestigious Book of the Year award from at least one of the following chess book reviewers.

  • British Chess Federation (BCF), English Chess Federation (ECF); awarded in October for the current year
  • ChessCafe.com; awarded in February for the previous year
  • Fred Cramer; joint award with Chess Journalists of America (CJA) 2002-2007; no award 2008?; awarded in August for the current year
  • Guardian News (guardian.co.uk); awarded in November for the current year
