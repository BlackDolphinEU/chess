Quiz 6.2(A)

1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 d6 3.d4 f5 4.dxe5 fxe4 5.Ng5 d5 6.Nc3 Bb4 7.e6

Black to move.

  • 7...Bxc3+ is bad because it opens the short diagonal for the Bishop on c1 : 8.bxc3 Nh6 9.Qh5+ Kf8 10.Ba3+ Kg8 11.Qf7+ Nxf7 12.exf7 mate
  • Better is 7...Nh6 8.Qh5+ Kf8 although Black is still in trouble.
