Do You Know How the Pieces Move? (Quiz no.6)
Match each piece with a legal move.

Choose your selection for a legal move (not necessarily a good move!) next to a piece/square combination in the left column. Your choice will be checked automatically in the right column to indicate it has already been used. When you're finished, click Check Your Score to see how well you've done!

Some pieces have more than one answer. Choose the best answer that allows you to match everything from both columns, one to one.
