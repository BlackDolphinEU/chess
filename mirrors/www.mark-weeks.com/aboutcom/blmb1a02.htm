Tactics!
Solutions to easy puzzles.

Click here for the puzzles without solutions.

No. 1

1.Qc3+ followed by 2.Qe1 forks the Black Rook and Bishop.

No. 2

1.Qd4 attacks the Black Knight and threatens mate.

No. 3

1.Qe4 attacks the Black Bishop a second time and threatens mate.

No. 4

1.Nxd3 and if 1...Rxd3 2.Ra8+ Kh7 3.Be4+ forks the King and Rook.

No. 5

1.Qc3 (a little better than 1.Qd4 Rxc2) forks the Black Rook and Knight.

No. 6

1.Nxd7 attacks the Black Queen and discovers an attack on the Rook at e8.

No. 7

1.Ng5 threatens mate and discovers an attack on the Black Bishop.
